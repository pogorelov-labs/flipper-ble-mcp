| **EEVBlog / RF teardown channels** | search per-topic | Deep electronics/RF context when you want the why, not the Flipper UI. |

> The Flipper scene moves fast and individual creators come and go — treat any single channel as a starting point, not gospel. **Cross-check tutorials against the current firmware UI** (menus get reorganized between releases).

## How to find current, up-to-date content
- On YouTube, **sort by upload date** and add the year (e.g. "Momentum starter guide 2026") — prefer the most recent dated walkthrough over top-ranked older ones.
- For CFW specifics, the **Telegram/Discord pinned messages** are more current than any video.
- For "does this still work?", search **r/flipperzero** + the firmware repo **issues/releases** before trusting a tutorial.
- The **awesome-flipperzero** master list and `github.com/topics/flipperzero` (sorted recently-updated) surface new tools faster than video.
